Red Bean Buns

Ingredients

  • sweet potatoes
  • glutinous rice powder
  • red bean paste

Steps

  1. Peel sweet potatoes
  2. Soften mixture by steaming or microwave. Make into a paste.
  3. Add glutinous rice powder to paste and mix.
  4. Add sweet red bean paste filling. Make into a bun.
  5. Coat both sides of the bun with a dash of oil.
  6. Preheat oven to 350-400.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes. Flip at halftime.
