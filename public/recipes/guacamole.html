Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocadoes
  • 1 roma tomato
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 jalepeno
  • 2 limes
  • cilantro

Steps

  1. Cube avocadoes
  2. Chop onions, tomato, cilantro, and jalepeno
  3. Mix everything
  4. Juice limes
  5. Add salt
