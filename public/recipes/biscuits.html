Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of baking powder
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425.
  2. Mix flour, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Cut butter into pats and add to flour.
  4. Mix in milk.
  5. Fold dough on a well-floured surface into a rough rectangle about an inch thick. Fold it over and gently pat it down again. Repeat. Cover the dough loosely with a kitchen towel.
  6. Cut dough into 12 pieces and place on a cookie sheet and bake until golden brown, approximately 10 to 15 minutes
